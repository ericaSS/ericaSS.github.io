Research works

I currently work in SABOC lab of NJIT. The SABOC (Structural Analysis of Biomedical Ontologies Center) lab, located in Department of Computer Science at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), is devoted to research exploring structural issues in biomedical ontologies (e.g., SNOMED CT, NCIt, NDF-RT, and ChEBI). Our research interests include ontology quality assurance methodologies,ontology summarization techniques, ontology change analysis, and analysis of families of ontologies.

Recently, I'm working with MDs and a Linguist on constructing an hybrid auto-review system (rule-base combined NLP method) for improving the effectiveness of medical terminoloyg manual auditing process.
